Our Story

Born from a Love of
Exceptional Cloth

VELOUR was founded in 2010 by designer Ananya Rao in a small Mumbai atelier. Starting with a single silk blouse that sold out in three days, the brand has grown into an internationally recognised luxury label with flagship stores in Mumbai, Delhi, Dubai, and London.

Every VELOUR piece is designed in-house and produced in limited quantities. We work exclusively with certified artisan mills in India, Italy, and Japan — sourcing only materials that meet our standards for quality, ethics, and environmental responsibility.

Hand-finished in certified Indian ateliers
Deadstock & GOTS-certified organic fabrics
Carbon-neutral shipping & responsible packaging
Made-to-order and bespoke service available

The VELOUR Approach

Our Process Is
Our Promise

01

Concept

Every collection begins with a narrative — a season's mood expressed through colour stories, cultural references, and tactile textures explored in our design studio.

02

Material

We source only the finest certified fabrics from partner mills in India, Italy, and Japan — selecting for drape, hand-feel, and sustainable provenance.

03

Construction

Each piece is cut, sewn, and finished by hand in our Mumbai atelier. We run no fast-fashion production lines. Quality over quantity, always.

04

Release

Collections launch in strictly limited quantities — each piece individually numbered. Once sold, it is never reproduced.
