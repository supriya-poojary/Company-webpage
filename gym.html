Who We Are

Built for Those Who Refuse to Settle

IRONHAUS was founded by a team of professional athletes and sports scientists with one mission: build a facility worthy of serious performers. Not a big-box gym. Not a boutique class studio. A real performance center.

Our 18,000 sqft flagship facility houses Olympic lifting platforms, a full powerlifting section, a dedicated combat sports zone, functional training rigs, recovery suites, and nutrition consulting — all under one roof.
